You play as Milo, a young wizard who lives with his mentor in the forest.

During a spellcasting session, Milo accidentally uses a powerful spell that curses his mentor and makes him ill.

While looking for a way to fix it, Milo discovers an old, damaged book that mentions a ritual cure requiring magical orbs hidden in different dimensions.

Now, Milo must travel to those worlds, collect the orbs, and try to save his mentor.
